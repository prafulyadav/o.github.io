WHO WE ARE AND WHAT DO WE DO?

We are a group of people, hailed from different fields of career, who have come together to create a new experience of volunteering. We are commited to create different opportunities for youth, provide them platform to discover their talents, and engage them in the right direction of career. We provide solutions to different day to day issues of our life and conduct different skills development training and capacity building workshop to enhance the personality. Our main objective is to strengthen the National Service Scheme (NSS) units, NYKS, and other youth led organizations (NGOs/Clubs) in +2 schools and colleges across the country.

WE TEACH THEM THE RIGHT WAY

TRPWS is a joint programme with the mission to empower and support underprivileged chidren by ensuring that every child has at least 1 adult assigned to them.
